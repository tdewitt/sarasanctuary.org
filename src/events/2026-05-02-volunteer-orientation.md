Our monthly volunteer orientation. Required for all new volunteers before they can start helping at the sanctuary.

We'll cover safety, what to expect, and the various ways you can contribute your time. Plan to spend 2–3 hours onsite. Wear close-toed shoes and comfortable clothes you don't mind getting dirty. Bring a water bottle.

Minimum age 16 (14 with a parent).
