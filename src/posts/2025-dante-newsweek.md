Dante is one of our international rescues. He lost a leg before he made it to us, and he traveled from Lebanon to the United States to find a safe place to land. That safe place turned out to be SARA.

Newsweek covered his journey and his life at the sanctuary. He's thriving on three legs, soaking up sun, and has claimed his favorite spot near the barn.
