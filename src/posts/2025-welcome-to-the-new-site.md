We rebuilt our website. Same SARA, same mission, cleaner experience.

## What's new

- **Faster pages.** No more waiting for huge pages to load when you just want to donate.
- **Real content.** All of our animals, tributes, and events are here — migrated from the old site.
- **Easier to update.** We can post news and announcements in minutes instead of hours.
- **Mobile-friendly.** Most of our visitors are on phones. The site now works well there.

## What's the same

The mission. Unconditional life for all animals, regardless of species, health, or temperament. 750 animals still live here on 128 acres of Central Texas prairie. We still rely on donors, volunteers, and a small dedicated team to keep this place running.

Thanks for sticking with us. [Donate](/give/), [adopt](/adopt/), or [come visit](/visit/).
