Amplify Austin is Central Texas's 24-hour day of giving. This year, we set a goal of $20,000. Thanks to an incredible community of donors, **we raised $31,100** for the animals at SARA.

Every dollar of that total goes directly to feed, vet care, and the day-to-day needs of our 750 residents. Horses, donkeys, alpacas, goats, pigs, chickens, ducks, dogs, cats, and a whole lot of other animals thank you.

## Where the money goes

- Feed: hay, grain, kibble, scratch
- Vet care: spay/neuter, emergency surgeries, chronic care
- Facility: fencing, barn maintenance, pasture rotation
- Unexpected: every sanctuary has them

## Missed it?

You can still support the animals year-round. [Donate](/give/), [sponsor a resident](/sponsor/), or [send something from our wish list](/give/#wishlist).
