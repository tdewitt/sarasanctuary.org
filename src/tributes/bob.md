During an Alternative Spring Break visit, Catherine, one of the participating students, met Bob, a loving and huge Great Pyrenees at SARA. After persuading her dad to adopt him, we sent Bob off to Philadelphia where he became "the King of the Wissahickon and at home." He lived many years as part of the Domanski family who now have two rescue dogs, Toby and Penny.
