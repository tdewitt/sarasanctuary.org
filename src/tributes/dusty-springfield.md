In loving memory of our sweet, beautiful, deaf girl, Dusty Springfield. She was the best dog, and we were lucky to have her in our lives and lucky to have SARA and Tracy there to help us when we were in times of need with her. So grateful to have her back for the last year of her loving life. We miss her every day. SARA Sanctuary is a godsend and a special haven for all of the world's creatures.
