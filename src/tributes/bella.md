My beloved family member, cat Bella, died on January 23, 2019. She was 15. She died from salivary cancer. Bella's nickname was "Queenie." She travelled to Texas with me, her human mom, and her 3 cat brothers from California. She adjusted (somewhat) to her 5 Texas sisters and brother, four of which were born feral and one who was abandoned by a neighbor. Bella was super cuddly, talkative and beautiful. Sleek black short hair and golden eyes. I miss her terribly and keep looking for her until I remember she is gone from this life.

Love from your human mom, Eugenie Baranoff
