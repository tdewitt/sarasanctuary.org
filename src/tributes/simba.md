How fortunate we are to have had Simba in our life! He greeted us every morning with a tap dance and little circles that filled us with joy. He taught us unconditional love, and no amount of shenanigans ever derailed the love we had for him. He loved going on walks, bike rides, and hikes and would always put a smile on people's face. During his senior years, he sometimes had a burst of energy on cold days when he would start racing us playfully and we just burst out in laughter at his puppy-like way to run. We miss him dearly.
