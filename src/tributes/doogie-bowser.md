Doogie Bowser joined the family in 2009. Over the course of 16 years, Doogie brought laughs, joy, and happiness to everyone who met him. He was loved and adored by his human mom and fur siblings alike.

Doogie's favorite pastimes included patrolling his backyard, housekeeping with his mom, and chasing his treat ball around the house. Doogie will be forever missed by all who knew him, including his family in Chicago, who loved to visit with him. He will be missed every day.

We love you, Doogie Bowser!
