Our sweet Elsa crossed the Rainbow Bridge on April 27, 2025. Elsa blessed our lives for over ten years, traveling across the country multiple times without complaint and always ready for the next adventure (even though she'd prefer to just sleep on the couch). The embodiment of a pure soul, Elsa never met someone she didn't love and couldn't wait to give kisses. Even 100 years would not have been enough time with our loving Elsa, but we know we will meet again someday.

Forever in our hearts, the sweetest girl.
