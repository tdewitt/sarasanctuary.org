Fiona was the light of her owner's life. Since the day she came home (teeny tiny and riding in a laundry basket), Fiona was playful, spunky, loving and always ready to greet guests at the door.

She lived her 14 long years with her doting brother, Doogie. She will always be remembered for her beautiful soft fur, her playful nature, and most of all, the love she showered upon her mother.

R.I.P. sweet Fiona. All of the other Nagy and Nylen family dogs are waiting on the other side of the Rainbow Bridge for you.
