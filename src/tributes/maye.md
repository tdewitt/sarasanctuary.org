Maye lived at SARA for seven years. She hit the jackpot to spend her time there with Tracy and her staff while waiting for a forever family.

Maye made her way up to the Chicago area through a rescue transport where my sister met her at an adoption show. She quickly knew she had to make Maye part of her family.

Maye lived almost two years in Chicago with her human mom and dad and a (fun-loving but sometimes slightly annoying) younger dog brother. She was so loved and is greatly missed. We hope you found a comfortable sunny spot in dog heaven, Maye!
