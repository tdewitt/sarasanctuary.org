Pokie was adopted from a Corpus Christi shelter over a decade ago and was a beloved member of the Harris family. He could frequently be found closely inspecting the floors for any discarded food; playing with his puppy, Joy; cuddling in bed with his dog mom; and receiving kisses on the snout from his tiny human, River. He was a loving, ever-present companion and will be deeply missed.
